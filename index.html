Pastor Jose Victor


"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms. If anyone speaks, they should do so as one who speaks the very words of God. If anyone serves, they should do so with the strength God provides, so that in all things God may be praised through Jesus Christ."

Pastor Jose Victor is a devoted servant of God who has dedicated his life to spreading the message of the gospel and reaching out to those in need.

Born and raised in Chennai, he has been blessed with a heart full of compassion and a deep love for God's word. With his TV ministry on Subhsandesh TV, he has touched the lives of countless people with his inspiring sermons and teachings.

Driven by his passion for outreach, he regularly visits slums in Mumbai and deep tribal areas in India to share the love of Christ and provide support to those in need. He pastors a church in Mumbai where he preaches the word of God with great zeal and dedication.

Pastor Jose Victor is not just a preacher, but also a coach and mentor for other pastors. He provides leadership training and guidance to help them become effective leaders in their communities.
